A Letter For You

written from the heart

💌
Dearest Shreya,

Thank you for choosing me and for making me a part of your life.

I can never forget those days when you used to secretly talk to me even when your family was around. I used to wait for your messages every single day — and one message from you was enough to bring a smile to my face.

I can never forget Borade Sir's classes, because somehow those classes brought us even closer. That little smile on your face whenever you saw my message... and the way your family used to ask, "Why are you smiling so much?" — only we know the reason behind those smiles.

Your efforts to meet me, sitting behind me on my bike, your hand resting on my shoulder, our walks on the hills, our conversations and all those little moments — they were so precious.

If I start writing about our memories, the day will end and my words still won't be enough. Every single moment with you was beautiful. I never thought a day would come when we would become so distant.

No matter what happens, those memories will always stay close to my heart.

I know things are not the same between us right now, and I know you are hurt. But I could never let your birthday pass without telling you how grateful I am for all the moments we shared.

Take care of yourself, Shreya. Always keep smiling. 🤍

Love you, Shreya — Rohit ❤️
